Bouldering in Cyprus

Bouldering can be regarded as still being relatively in its infancy in Cyprus, experiencing a boom over the past few years.
For the sake of those who are not familiar with the sport, bouldering is a form of rock climbing that is performed without the use of ropes or harnesses. Instead, climbers use crash pads to protect themselves from falls.
It is a great way to build strength, improve your climbing technique, test your limits and have fun with friends.
All while spending time outdoors and enjoying the beautiful Cyprus landscape in and around the Akamas Peninsula.

Why Cyprus?

With its stunning landscapes, warm climate, and friendly locals, Cyprus is the perfect destination for climbers of all levels.
Whether you're looking for easy problems or hard projects, bouldering in Cyprus has something for everyone, with around 700 bouldering problems ranging from f3 (V0) to f8A (V11).
This area on the western coast of Cyprus offers a wide range of bouldering spots scattered across the hills and valleys, from small to huge boulders, offering various styles of climbing on quality sandstone rock.

Ideal Winter Destination or Visiting with Family

Unlike many other climbing destinations in Europe, Cyprus offers ideal climbing conditions during the winter months, when northern Europe is usually covered in snow, rain and freezing temperatures.
With sunny weather and mild temperatures, you can enjoy your climbing sessions in comfortable conditions while even hitting the beach under the winter sun, making it a great sunny winter getaway for climbers.
Cyprus is known for its hospitality, friendly locals, food, culture and history, making it a great destination for visiting with your family. Short distances and easy access means that you can easily combine climbing with other activities in the Paphos region or the even the rest of the island.
