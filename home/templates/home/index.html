Welcome to Bouldering Cyprus

Bouldering Cyprus is the ultimate guide to the best bouldering spots in Cyprus. Discover the most popular climbing areas, find detailed route descriptions, and explore stunning photography. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced climber, Bouldering Cyprus has something for everyone.

What is Bouldering?

Bouldering is a form of rock climbing that is performed without the use of ropes or harnesses. Instead, climbers use crash pads to protect themselves from falls. Bouldering is a great way to build strength, improve your climbing technique, and have fun with friends.

Why Bouldering Cyprus?

Cyprus is home to some of the best bouldering spots in the world. With its stunning landscapes, warm climate, and friendly locals, Cyprus is the perfect destination for climbers of all levels. Whether you're looking for easy problems or hard projects, Bouldering Cyprus has something for everyone.
